The Development

Here you can read about the development and what it took to make this game happen.

Basic information

This adventure fantasy game allows you to explore a 3D low-poly world from 3rd person perspective with no internet connection required after the download from git.

Who we are

This game was developed as a student project in the bachelor program of computer science at the University of Tübingen. The developers are: Stephan Amann, Cornelius Bopp, David Kleindiek, Tanja Huber, Tina Truong and Amelie Schäfer.

What we did

  • Intricate Systems for Quests, Inventory and Character Upgrading
  • Particles and Light Design
  • Extensive Combat System with Multiple Attack Modes, Advanced Movement and Customised Animations
  • Adapted Sounds and Selfmade Soundtrack
  • AI for all Non Player Characters
  • Design of the Logo and all Icons within the Game
  • Fully Functional HUD and Interactive Popups
  • Detailed Openworld Map
  • Fully Low Poly Terrain, Water and Structures
  • Extensive Playtesting and Balancing

Sources of Inspiration

Ryan Laley, Matt Aspland, CGHOW, PrismaticaDev

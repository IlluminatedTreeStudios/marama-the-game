The Features of the Game

Here you can read about the features, that make this game so special.

Light leads the way

In this world the caretaking of light is crucial for survival. Your life, especially, is so closely connected to light, that it depends on it. Thus, roaming too deep into the darkness will take more and more of a toll on you! But you’re not left all alone in the dark - trees can charge you up until you’re glowing as bright as ever again.
Furthermore, if you plan to shed light on the mysteries lying in the shadows beyond, you are sure to encounter shadow creatures. To defeat them, you need to spend energy on lightning attacks. Should you manage to take them down and restore the balance, you will be given energy back.


Solve Complex Quests

While exploring the world you have the possibility to interact with and get to learn about various folks. Aid them and you are sure to gain quite a prestigious reputation, as they will remember your impact on their lives! A variety of thought-through complex stories and interactions are awaiting for you to find them!


Become A Collector

Our world is filled with items and treasure chests! Collected loot can grant various temporary buff effects, healing, or more insight in the world through captivating stories.Our world is filled with items and treasure chests! Collected loot can be utilized in various situations. During battle items can be used to buff regeneration or damage. Traversing the world some can be used to trade with merchants or grant more insight into the world through captivating stories.


Explore Astonishing and Mystical Landscapes

Find yourself captivated by our handcrafted low-poly high-quality world with much to investigate. Be it fascinating landscapes, interactions with compelling people, or the mysteries behind the struggle for a light in the darkness.

Experience Excellent Movement and Combat Behaviour

During combat you have various options in regards to fighting distance with a melee as well as ranged mode, as well as movement with evasion, rolls and dodges. There’s even more adaptation possible through the skill system.


Improve Your Abilities and Skills

Through leveling up and learning more about the world, so does your character learn more about its puzzling powers. By using skill points gained from leveling you can unlock new skills allowing for a unique gaming experience for every player. Possible customization options are sorted in four categories covering darkness, health, combat and speed. Furthermore included in the skill system is the option to gain completely new attacks.


Enjoy The Intricate Lighting Details

We decided to create own particles with the intention to give the game more of our own buzz – with fireflies living around the glowing trees. Those little buddies also seem to follow you around, strangely. But it seems there’s always a spark to guide your way in the darkness.
Not only that, but they also seem to aid you in overcoming struggles, and mainly enemies. But of course, you’re in the spotlight and able to create your very own lightning show: the various projectiles all have different looks, and every hit comes with a spark!
